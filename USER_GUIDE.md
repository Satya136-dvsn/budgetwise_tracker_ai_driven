# BudgetWise Tracker - User Guide

Welcome to **BudgetWise Tracker**, your personal finance companion designed to help you track expenses, manage budgets, and achieve your financial goals with the power of AI.

## ðŸš€ Getting Started

### 1. Registration

1. Navigate to the **Register** page.
2. Enter your **Full Name**, **Email**, and a secure **Password**.
3. Click **Register**. You will be automatically logged in.

### 2. Initial Setup

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**.
2. Set your **Monthly Income** and **Savings Target**.
3. Choose your preferred **Currency** (Default: USD).
4. Click **Save Changes**.
